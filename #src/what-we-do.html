Whether it’s difficult situations with elements of distress, retirement situations, or the sale of a business, the Amcomri team brings extensive financial management, process optimisation and general commercial skills to smoothly transition to new ownership. We’re on-hand to help new group members optimise performance and improve operating processes.

There are a variety of different scenarios where we might step in such as when a business finds itself in a difficult situation with an element of distress, they’re in need of extra assistance, or perhaps the owner is looking to retire. Whatever it may be, our principal goal is to instil stability through operational expertise and financial management that leads to positive growth.

our approach

  • we understand the business the people
    • Hands on working in the business and the with the retiring owner.
    • Define the potential succession management need.
    • Measure and reinforce successes.
  • understand the key risks and opportunities
    • Define these and ensure they are addressed in our initial planning work
    • Define the resources needed and timescales to ensure a successful ownership transition
    • Apply a 'fresh pair of eyes' to the business
  • plan and implement the transition from the previous owner / manager
    • Prepare a time based transition plan to delivery the joint objectives - typically using 90 day plan principles;
    • Commence appropriate recruitment with the benefit of having worked to understand the needs of the business first;
  • post transition - develop value
    • Identify and support organic growth opportunities through product or market extension;
    • Identify and acquire complementary bolt-on acquisitions.

PARTNERSHIP IS PIVOTAL

Our team comprises of many skilled people such as industrial experts and operational specialists, but perhaps just as importantly, we’re collaborators. We’ll work closely with any company we acquire or invest in to optimise performance and improve operating processes.

Our aim is to create a stable environment for everyone, at every level, within the business to thrive. When businesses become a member of our group, we’ll help them get to where they want to be through financial support, but more importantly, they’ll be able to tap into a considerable wealth of commercial expertise they can rely on now, and in the future.
